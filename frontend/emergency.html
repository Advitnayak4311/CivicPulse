When to Use Emergency Report?

Use this channel exclusively for municipal issues presenting immediate public safety hazards.

Road Cave-in / Crater

Severe potholes or road collapse that can swallow vehicles or cause fatal traffic accidents.

Sewage Overflow

Severe blockages causing toxic wastewater to flood into residential spaces or blocking arterial streets.

Fallen Tree / Hazard

Large trees or heavy branches fully obstructing streets, crushing vehicles, or hanging on high-voltage power lines.

Exposed Live Wires

Sparks, snapped electrical poles, or exposed cables lying on puddles or footpaths creating immediate electrocution risk.

Water Main Burst

Major municipal pipeline ruptures resulting in massive road flooding, structural damage, or severe local water supply cuts.

Structural Collapse

Damaged overhead bridges, public buildings, or retaining walls showing signs of imminent structural collapse.

Important Notice: For police, fire, or medical emergencies, please dial 112 immediately. This quick portal is reserved for reporting critical civic infrastructure hazards only.
